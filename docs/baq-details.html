Migration of the control system for the Beer Elaboration Area

This project involved the migration of brewing, fermentation, and services areas in the brewing facility. The migration was accomplished through the configuration and programming of Siemens S7-400 PLCs. The process automation was developed using Simatic Step7 and Braumat 7.1.
It was necessary to carry out a start-up in record time to return the factory to its normal operation and achieve the productivity it had prior to the system modification.

I programmed most of the fermentation sequences and led the start-up of this area.
Designed and configured route control strategies to optimize fluid transfer throughout the production process.
Supported the implementation of brewing section migration, offering technical troubleshooting throughout the start-up phase.
Successfully managed the complex challenge of concurrent process testing, monitoring, and optimization within stringent time constraints.
Maintained continuous, proactive communication with factory personnel to identify and address critical production process requirements.
Delivered comprehensive training programs for maintenance staff on new technological implementations.
