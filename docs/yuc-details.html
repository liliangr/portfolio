Automation of the Brewing Process for a New Brewery

Cervecería Yucateca was built as a greenfield project, featuring a new brewing facility with a 1,200 hl brewing capacity per batch and advanced fermentation and filtration systems. The process automation was implemented using S7-400 series PLCs programmed with Simatic Step 7 and Braumat 7.0. The cold block sections incorporated advanced route control strategies to optimize fluid transfer operations.

In this project, I succesfully:
Developed and implemented complex fermentation sequences.
Led extensive commissioning sessions while simultaneously managing new process startups.
Collaborated with process engineers and technicians from multiple companies to optimize sequences.
Executed hands-on plant operations throughout the critical initial startup phase.
Delivered comprehensive training programs for new operations and maintenance personnel on the newly implemented technology.
Maintained ongoing communication with plant management to coordinate new process startups.
Provided round-the-clock support for the production system, ensuring smooth operations and rapid problem resolution.
